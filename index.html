Welcome to Werewolf!

Welcome to Werewolf, the popular social detective game of lycanthropy and lynching!

"Werewolf" is a popular social game originally conceived as a modification of the party game "Mafia." It has since grown to include many unique roles and mechanics. You can join us by using an IRC client to connect to irc.coldfront.net on port 6667, and joining #werewolf.
